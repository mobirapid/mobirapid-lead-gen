## Become a Mobirapid City Partner

**Represent Mobirapid in your city. Handle open-box deliveries and warranty support. Earn ₹2,000 per device — up to ₹1 lakh a month.**

Mobirapid sells certified refurbished MacBooks, phones and tablets across India. What makes us different is **Open Box Delivery** — our customers don't pay until they've inspected their device in person. That promise only works if we have a trusted representative in each city.

That representative could be you.

[ Apply to partner → ]

---

## What a City Partner does

- **Receive and hold devices** dispatched to you for customers in your city
- **Visit the customer** at a scheduled time — home or office
- **Conduct the open-box handover**: open the sealed device in front of the customer, let them inspect and test it
- **Collect payment to Mobirapid** (never in your personal account) and hand over the device
- **Accept returns on the spot** if the customer finds a functional issue
- **Provide first-line warranty support** — collect devices needing service and coordinate with our team

No shop required. No technical repair skills required. We train you.

---

## What you earn

**₹2,000 per device, or 2% of its value — whichever is higher.**

So every delivery pays you a minimum of ₹2,000, and high-value devices pay more (a ₹1,80,000 MacBook Pro pays ₹3,600).

| Devices delivered / month | Your earnings |
|---|---|
| 5 | ₹10,000 |
| 15 | ₹30,000 |
| 30 | ₹60,000 |
| 50 | **₹1,00,000** |

That's roughly **two deliveries a day** to reach ₹1 lakh a month.

*Earnings depend on order volume in your city and the deliveries you complete. Mobirapid does not guarantee a minimum income — new partners usually start in the lower rows and grow as their city's demand builds.*

**Paid monthly**, against a statement of every delivery you completed.

## Partner fees — stated plainly

| | Amount | Refundable? |
|---|---|---|
| **Partner fee (one-time)** | ₹25,000 | **No** |
| **Security deposit** | ₹2,00,000 | **Yes** — fully refundable |

**What the ₹25,000 partner fee covers:** onboarding and training, your welcome kit, partner ID and authorisation, access to our partner systems, branding material, and ongoing support. It is a one-time fee and is **not refundable**.

**Why there's a security deposit:** you will personally hold devices worth several lakhs at a time. The ₹2,00,000 deposit secures those assets while they're in your custody. It is **not an investment and earns no return.** It is **refunded in full** when your partnership ends, once all devices and materials in your possession have been returned and accounts settled — normally within 15 days. Deductions are only made against devices lost, damaged, or unaccounted for while in your custody.

**Important:** these amounts are payable only to Mobirapid's company account after your partner agreement is signed. Never pay any individual. Please read the agreement carefully — and have it reviewed independently — before you commit.

## How to become a partner

**1. Apply** — fill in the short form below with your name, number and city.

**2. Interview** — our team calls you for a short interview. We discuss the role in full, answer every question, and assess suitability. Selection is on merit, and we appoint a limited number of partners per city.

**3. Verification** — basic KYC and address verification (Aadhaar/PAN).

**4. Agreement** — we sign a formal partner agreement covering responsibilities, commission, deposit terms and exit conditions. You get a copy and time to review it.

**5. Onboarding** — pay the fees, complete training, receive your welcome kit.

**6. Start earning** — your first delivery is supervised, and support stays with you.

## FAQ

**Do I need a shop or office?** No. You need a secure place to store devices and reliable local transport.

**Do I need technical knowledge?** No. We train you on inspection and handover. Repairs are handled by our team.

**Is this a full-time commitment?** No. Most partners work flexible hours around studies, family or another job.

**When do I get my security deposit back?** In full, within 15 days of your partnership ending, once all devices and materials are returned and accounts are settled.

**Can I lose my deposit?** Only through devices lost, damaged, or unaccounted for while in your custody. It isn't at market risk.

**How many partners per city?** A limited number, so existing partners get enough volume to make it worthwhile.

**When do I get paid?** Monthly, with a statement of every delivery you completed.
