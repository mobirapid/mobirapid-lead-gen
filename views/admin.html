Payment gateway — PayU

Turn this on to accept the booking/reserve amount online. When enabled, the "Reserve" button opens PayU secure checkout (the "Payment link" above is ignored). Your keys are stored securely and never shown on the website.

"Book with ₹X" button on every product

The booking amount is the % of the sale price or the minimum, whichever is higher — computed per product. It goes straight to PayU payment.

The button only appears when PayU is turned ON above (or a fixed payment link is set) and the product has a price. For the dynamic per-product amount, enter your PayU Merchant Key + Salt above and set "PayU enabled" to On.

If set, the Reserve button opens this link directly. Leave blank to use the built-in PayU checkout (which also captures the buyer's details).
